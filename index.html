Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web design services, pricing, and process. Can't find the answer? Contact us.

How much does a website cost in South Africa?
Website costs in South Africa vary based on complexity. Basic small business websites start from R6,999, e-commerce sites from R29,999, and custom solutions vary. We offer flexible payment plans with a 50% deposit to start and balance on completion.
How long does it take to build a website?
Most websites launch within 2 weeks. Basic websites take 1-2 weeks, e-commerce sites 3-5 weeks, and custom enterprise projects 6-10 weeks.
Do your websites work on mobile devices?
Absolutely! All our websites are fully mobile-responsive and optimized for South Africa's diverse device market. Mobile-first design is our standard approach.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept PayPal (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, or American Express), WhatsApp payment arrangements, and EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) via FNB (First National Bank). We require a 50% deposit to start with the balance due on completion.
Do you offer SEO services?
Yes! We specialize in SEO services for South African businesses. Our local SEO optimization helps you rank on Google.co.za for keywords like 'web design Cape Town', 'web design Johannesburg', and other South African search terms.
